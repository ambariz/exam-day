# Exam Day

A silly visual novel about surviving the most dangerous event known to students: **An exam.**

Everyone has advice. Your friend has advice. Your teacher has advice. That one random guy has advice. Most of it is terrible.

Can you make it through Exam Day?

[![Click to Play](https://img.shields.io/badge/CLICK%20ME%20TO%20PLAY-itch.io-red?style=for-the-badge&logo=itchdotio)](https://ambariz.itch.io/exam-day)

**LINK :** https://ambariz.itch.io/exam-day

---

## Gameplay

- Enter your name
- Make choices throughout the day
- Decide who to trust
- Try not to destroy your Grades, Energy, and Sanity
- Take the final exam
- Unlock different endings

---

## Features

- Funny visual novel
- Multiple choices
- Multiple endings
- Name input
- Background music
- Persistent ending collection
---

## Endings

- Topper Ending
- Sleep Champion Ending
- Chaos Ending
- Average Student Ending

## Warning
